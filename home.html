About Us

At TechEdu, We provide career counseling and professional advice before you choose a particular career path. We perform intensive counseling to determine aptitude, strength and weakness before enrolling students in professional training program. We take great pleasure in the fact that our counseling service has helped students and professionals to succeed in their professional endeavors. We strongly believe in the power of self-analysis and mainly focus on motivating the users to choose there career on their own better judgements and insights. We also provide a platform for people of similar intersets to come together and share their views without the fear of judgement and keep them updated with the recent events in their respective filed.





Mission

Motivating the students to analyze their own choices without being influenced. Bringing people with similar interests together and providing an interface for them to communicate along with recent updates in their field.

Vision

We are highly motivated by the vision of enhancing professionalism in the any field beyond the national boundaries. We ensure that learners are imparted with the highest quality in the courses they undertake.

Audience

We cater the students who are unsure about their career choices and want a platform to share their views with people sharing similar interests. Our audience mostly includes career concerned parents and students.
